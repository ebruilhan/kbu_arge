Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the purpose of the R&D Club?

Our goal is to support students in developing projects related to science, technology, and innovation.

2. Who can join the club?

All Karabuk University students are welcome to join, regardless of their department.

3. How can I become a member?

You can fill out the membership form on the “Join Us” page or contact us via email.

4. Do I need experience to participate in projects?

No experience is required. We support beginners and encourage learning through teamwork.
